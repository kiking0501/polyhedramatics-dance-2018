[ Maths x Music]

A Music Clock in terms of A Regular 12-gon

A Music Clock

Bar 0: Notes from the Db Major scale 

    有趣諗法:

    一個有住 24 條對稱線嘅正十二面體 dodecahedron (12-gon), 喺數學上可以用 Dihedral Group of Order 24 (Dih_12) 去表達;  而如果我地將一個 octave 睇做 12 個 semitones 呢?

    一啲常用嘅 music actions: 例如 transposition, invertion, major / minor triads, PLR-group (from Neo-Riemannian theory), 其實可以一一對應番 group actions, 令呢個 Music Clock 成為一個數學上 well defined 嘅 Dih_12!

    Reference: Musical Actions of Dihedral Groups, Alissa S. Crans, Thomas M. Fiore, and Ramon Satyendra, 2008
